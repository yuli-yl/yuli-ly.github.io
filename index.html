Biography

Yu LI is a fourth-year Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shatin, Hong Kong). She received her Master degree in 2017 from Katholieke Universiteit Leuven (KU Leuven, Leuven, Belgium). Before that, she received the B.Eng degree in 2016 from both KU Leuven and University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC, Chengdu, China).

Currently, her research interest includes AI security and AI testing.
